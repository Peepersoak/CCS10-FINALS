Tidal Energy

Entry

A way to prevent air pollution is to resort and invest in renewable energy.


Tidal energy is one of the types of renewable energy. Its source of energy comes from the surge of ocean waters through the rise and fall of tides and currents, also known as tidal flows, and generate usable electricity.


One of the process of producing tidal energy consists of utilizing turbines that are positioned in tidal streams which are fast-flowing body of water produced by tides. Turbines create a steady, reliable flow of electricity.


Unlike the harmful-traditional way of producing electricity, tidal power is a source of clean and renewable energy that does not emit greenhouse gases. It is practically used for large, commercial-scale projects.

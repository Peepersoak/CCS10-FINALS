Solar Energy

Entry

A way to reduce air pollution is to resort and invest in renewable energy.


A popular option for renewable energy is solar energy. Its source of energy is light emitted directly from the sun which is also known as electromagnetic radiation. The process consists of the sun producing heat which then causes chemical reaction and generates electricity that is essential for homes and the industries.


For solar energy to take effect, solar panels are utilized. Solar panels consist of photovoltaics (PV) cells which absorbs the energy from the light that the sun produces. That energy generates a direct current (DC) that is converted into an alternating current (AC) through an inverter which provides power to appliances.


By adapting to the utilization of solar energy, this reduces the production of electricity through the traditional method which consists of burning of coal, oil, and gas that promote harm to the environment. Therefore, it lessens carbon footprint and greenhouse gases globally and slows down climate change.

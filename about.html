Who we are

We are a 1st year students of Angeles University Foundation taking up the course of Bachelors of Science in Information Technology. We are tasked to create an informative website on how to solve the air pollution.

In this website we decided to showcase and give you information on different alternatives that you can use to reduce our global emmision.

We provided 12 different alternatives that you can find in our Gallery.

Our Purpose

We are currently engage in a battle in which we need to reduce our global emission by half before 2030. Failure to do this will have an irreversible effects on our environment.

Therefore the primary purpose of this website as stated before is to inform the readers on how they can use different alternatives that can reduce our global emission.

Take note that there are other ways to reduce our global emmission. Simply turning off and unpluggin unused appliance can also help in reducing our global emmision. Instead of using your air conditioner unit to cool down your house, you can simply open your windows.
